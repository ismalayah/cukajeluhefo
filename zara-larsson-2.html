Zara Larsson popularly known as Zara Maria Larsson is a Swedish singer. As of 2022, Zara Larsson’s net worth is $12 million. She rose to fame as a pop musician for the record label “TEN Music Group” in 2012.

She has been working as a singer since 2008. She has released 3 studio albums, 4 EPs, 28 singles, and 24 music videos in her victorious career and has earned multiple awards and a marvelous net worth of about $12 million.

Her first original single “Uncover” gained a high ranking on the “Sverigetopplistan” chart and “DigiListan” chart and was also certified platinum in Sweden by “Universal Music Sweden”.

Her debut studio album titled “1” was launched in 2014, which was highly admired in Sweden as well as in other countries like the UK, Norway, Denmark, etc. She has been a part of several music concerts and tours. She performed at the “Nobel Peace Prize Concert” two times in 2013 and 2017.

Her most notable singles include “Uncover”, “Lush Life”, “Never Forget You”, “Ain’t My Fault” and “Symphony” which secured number one position on the “Swedish singles chart”.
