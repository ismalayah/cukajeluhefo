Who Is Shemar Moore's Girlfriend, Jesiree Dizon?

In the past, Shemar Moore was pretty open about his previous relationships. However, Moore's recent baby announcement caught his longtime fans off guard, partially because he hadn't publicly been in a relationship with anyone since splitting with his ex, actor Anabelle Acosta (via The Sun). However,  Page Six referred to Jesiree Dizon as Shemar's "longtime girlfriend." At this time, neither party has openly spoken out about the other on social media. However, they did appear rather joyous in their gender reveal video. Perhaps they may share more in the future. 

In the meantime, Moore is gearing up to become a first-time parent. But, as ET reports, Dizon is already a mother to two children from previous relationships. And while Dizon has yet to update her 43,000 Instagram followers on her current pregnancy, her feed is littered with adorable photos of her oldest children, including birthday posts, holiday hijinx, and sweet family portraits. As the outlet noted, Dizon was previously attached to actor, Stephen Bishop, with whom she shares her youngest child.
