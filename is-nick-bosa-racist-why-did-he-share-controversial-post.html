Is Nick Bosa Racist?

Nick Bosa was referred to as a racist as he referred to Kaepernick in his tweets as a “clown,” Black Panther as the “worst Marvel movie,” and Beyonce’s music as “complete trash.”

In his tweets, he also expressed support for President Donald Trump. Because he might be selected by the San Francisco 49ers later this month, he later deleted all of the tweets.

Examining Bosa’s Instagram history revealed that he liked several posts that used the N-word and a homophobic insult. This information was discovered by the Twitter account @RzstProgramming.

The posts were from John Harrington, who went to St. Thomas Aquinas in Florida with Bosa and were published in 2014.

One of the Instagram accounts to “like” the photo was Bosa’s account, nbsmallerbear.
