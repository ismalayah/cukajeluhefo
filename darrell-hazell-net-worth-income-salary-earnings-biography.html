Darrell Hazell Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Darrell Hazell (born April 14, 1964) is an American collegiate football coach who specialises in the sport of football. He now serves as the head football coach at Purdue. He previously served as the head coach at Kent State University from 2011 to 2012. Hazell is a native of Cinnaminson Township, New Jersey, and graduated in 1982 from Cinnaminson High School, where he participated in football and track. He then went on to attend Muskingum University, where he began playing football and track in the autumn of 1982. While at the school, he was a member of the football team, where he was a starter for his last three years. Hazell received his bachelor’s degree in 1986. Former assistant coaches at Oberlin, Eastern Illinois, Penn, Western Michigan, Army, West Virginia, and Rutgers are among others who have worked with him. From 2004 through 2010, Hazell was as the wide receivers coach at Ohio State, where he worked with Jim Tressel. In December 2010, he was appointed as the head coach at Kent State University. In a vote conducted by the Mid-American Conference’s coaches on November 28, 2012, Hazell was awarded Coach of the Year for the 2012 season. Purdue announced the hiring of Hazell as their new head coach on December 5, 2012, to take over for Danny Hope.
