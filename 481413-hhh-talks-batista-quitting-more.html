Brock Lesnar Selling His Home

According to CityPages.com, Brock Lesnar is selling a home in Maple Plain, Minnesota for $800,000. The 3,247 square foot home was built in 1996 and has four bedrooms and four bathrooms.

Triple H's Latest Sit-Down Interview

The following video is the latest sit-down interview with Triple H and Michael Cole. During the interview, HHH comments on Batista quitting WWE and why he chose Seth Rollins for Evolution.

In a quick recap, Triple H said Batista quit WWE because that's what Batista does, he quits. It wasn't the first time he quit WWE and it likely won't be the last. He said he hopes Batista's movie career works out because he knows Batista doesn't have a Plan B.

HHH said he recruited Seth Rollins to be in Evolution because Rollins has been the brains of The Shield since day one. He said Rollins "adapted" while The Shield "perished."

In related news, Batista's WWE profile has been removed and is now in the Alumni Section of WWE.com.
