Bray Wyatt Gets Cryptic During RAW With The Number 4, Reconnects With Luke Harper

Bray Wyatt has been off WWE television since him and “Woken” Matt Hardy have went their separate ways, but that hasn’t stopped the man not on brand to make some noise during RAW. Wyatt took to social media to make some cryptic Tweets heavily focused on the number four. He also mentioned an individual by the name of “Dr. Mercy” which coincidentally or not, was the last name of the WWE character Bray’s persona is heavily speculated to be based off of in Waylon Mercy (Dan Spivey).
